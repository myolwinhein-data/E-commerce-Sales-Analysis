# Project Title: E-commerce Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis


## Project Overview:
This project focuses on analyzing sales data to understand business performance and customer behavior. Using Advanced SQL, I extracted key metrics to help the business make better decisions regarding marketing, inventory, and revenue growth.

## Technical Skills Used (Advanced SQL):
- Complex Joins: To combine data from Customers, Products and Orders tables.
- Window Functions: For calculating cumulative sales (Running Total) and ranking products.
- CTEs & Subqueries: To organize complex logic and find repeat customers.
- Data Aggregation: To calculate total revenue and inventory turnover rates.


## Business Insights & Actions
- customer loyalty: Since 3 repeat customer drive core loyalty, should launch a loyalty program to give more favor on  them.
- Market Demand: With electronics products being the top category in all regions, so prioritize marketing campaigns and stock for this category to maximize sales.
- Revenue growth: As high value product are the main revenue drivers, we should let customer sell products together as a set to make them more affordable.
- Customer Retention: identified high clv to receive personalized promotions, ensuring long-term engagement with the brand.
- Inventory Efficiency: With electronics(48) being 1.5 times greater than appliances(32),increase stock for electronics to prevent stockouts and loss of revenue.
